DBaaS:
Pro:
- Easier to setup
- Maintenance done by provider 
- builtin backups 
- builtin security features
- availability ensurance by provider

Con:
- More costly
- Less flexible
- ecosystem lock in 


DIY
Pro:
- Cheaper
- Customizable
- Flexible

Cons:
- More complex setup and maintenance
- Need to roll your own backup strategy


If you can afford the setup and have the expertise then DIY is the better choice.
